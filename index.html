Empowering Rwanda's Future Leaders

The Girls Leaders Forum (GLF), based at the University of Rwanda, champions gender equality, youth development, and vital adolescent sexual and reproductive health (SRH) education.

Explore Our Initiatives

Our Core Pillars of Impact

Gender Equality

Working to dismantle barriers and promote equal opportunities for all young people in education and leadership.

Health & SRH

Delivering comprehensive SRH information to reduce teenage pregnancies and combat misinformation through dialogue.

Youth Leadership

Cultivating the next generation of ethical and effective leaders through mentorship, training, and community engagement projects.

Latest Campaign: Parental Voices

Our new initiative empowers parents to openly discuss SRH, combating stigma and misinformation at the family level.
